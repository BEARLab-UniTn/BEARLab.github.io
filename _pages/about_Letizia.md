Letizia Girardi is a PhD student in Engineering Science at the University of Trento, focusing on AI for medical imaging. Her research explores deep learning methods for image analysis and generation, multimodal diagnostic pipelines, and reliable, interpretable AI models to support clinical decision-making. She has worked on projects involving brain MRI analysis, computer vision for human motion, and adaptive AI architectures, and has collaborated with clinical and engineering teams on research initiatives in radiology and vascular surgery.
